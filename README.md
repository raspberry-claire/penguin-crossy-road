# 🐧 Penguin Crossy Road 🐧

A fun, blocky penguin-themed version of Crossy Road built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS!

## 🎮 How to Play

### Objective
Help your cute penguin waddle as far forward as possible while avoiding obstacles and the chasing eagle!

### Controls

**Desktop:**
- **Arrow Keys** - Move up/down/left/right
- **Spacebar** - Move forward (same as up arrow)

**Mobile:**
- **Swipe Up** - Move forward
- **Swipe Down** - Move backward
- **Swipe Left** - Move left
- **Swipe Right** - Move right
- **Tap** - Move forward

### Obstacles

1. **Roads** 🚗 - Avoid cars and trucks driving across the road
2. **Train Tracks** 🚂 - Watch out for fast-moving trains!
3. **Rivers** 🌊 - Jump on moving ice chunks to cross safely
4. **Seals** 🦭 - Avoid seals swimming in the water
5. **Natural Hazards** 🪨 - Dodge snow-covered rocks and trees
6. **The Eagle** 🦅 - If you don't move forward fast enough, an eagle will swoop down and catch you!

### Scoring

- Earn **1 point for every block** you move forward
- Moving backward won't give you extra points
- Try to beat your high score!

## 🎯 Difficulty

This version is balanced for all ages - easier than the original Crossy Road but still challenging!

## 🚀 Features

- ✅ Blocky, voxel-style graphics
- ✅ Multiple obstacle types (cars, trains, rivers, seals, hazards)
- ✅ Chasing eagle mechanic
- ✅ Score tracking with high score
- ✅ Desktop (keyboard) and mobile (touch) controls
- ✅ Smooth animations
- ✅ Game over screen with restart button
- ✅ Responsive design

## 📱 Play Online

Simply open `index.html` in your web browser and start playing!

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5** - Game structure and canvas
- **CSS3** - Styling and responsive design
- **Vanilla JavaScript** - Complete game logic, collision detection, and controls

## 💡 Tips

- Keep moving forward or the eagle will catch you!
- Time your movements carefully on roads and train tracks
- Jump on ice chunks to safely cross rivers
- Watch out for seals in the water!
- Natural obstacles block your path - navigate around them
